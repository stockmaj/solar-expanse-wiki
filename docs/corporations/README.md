# Corporations

The five playable starting factions in Solar Expanse. Each scenario
(Colonization Era / The Expansion / Race Beyond) ships a different
pre-built save where every corporation starts with its own completed
research, funding, and fleet. Difficulty further scales starting money
and ongoing costs.

_Prelude and Early-Exploration scenarios are procedural and not listed
in the comparison table — they have no pre-built save data, but they
do appear in the epoch timeline below._

## Epoch / Timeline

Solar Expanse ships five start epochs, each with its own in-game
start year and roster of playable corporations. The Prelude and Early
Exploration epochs are procedural (no pre-built save); the later three
ship the comparison saves below.

| Epoch | Start year | Playable corporations | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Prelude** | 1960 | Solex | Procedural; no pre-built save |
| **Early Exploration** | 2000 | CNSA, ESA, NASA, Roscosmos, Solex | Procedural; no pre-built save |
| **Colonization Era** | 2100 | CNSA, ESA, NASA, Roscosmos, Solex | Locked behind story progression |
| **The Expansion** | 2100 | CNSA, ESA, NASA, Roscosmos, Solex | Locked behind story progression |
| **Race Beyond** | 2300 | CNSA, ESA, NASA, Roscosmos, Solex | Locked behind story progression |

Difficulty also scales ongoing upkeep (Explorer ×0.5, Pioneer ×1, Veteran ×1.5) and supply usage by the same factors — not reflected in the table below, which only shows starting state.

## Corporations at a glance

### SoleX

At the forefront of the private sector in aerospace, SoleX has been making strides thanks to their first truly reusable launch vehicles. While they've undoubtedly revolutionised the industry, can they sustain their grand ambitions?

**Flavor traits (from new-game screen):**
● Starting research: Improved Reusable Rockets and Hydrolox engines
● Starts with additional fuel production

### NASA

As the oldest space agency still around, NASA boasts an impressive record in space exploration and research. Though they are the only ones to have landed humans on the moon they had a rather long lapse in deep space missions. But now with the lessons of old nuclear propulsion research finally applied in practice, they are in a prime position to take the lead in exploring the solar system.

**Flavor traits (from new-game screen):**
● Starting research: Nuclear Power and Propulsion
● Starts with hydrogen production

### ESA

Representing the collective of European space efforts, this agency has assembled the foremost minds of the continent to explore and research space. With cutting edge electric spacecraft and space infrastructure to build more, they are ready to go further beyond.

**Flavor traits (from new-game screen):**
● Starting research: Improved electric engines and computing technologies
● Starts with research lab

### CNSA

The last of the great powers to fully join the space race, China has managed to rapidly advance and become a peer of other space programs. With their industrial might, deep space colonization is within their grasp, and they are determined to not just match, but exceed everyone else.

**Flavor traits (from new-game screen):**
● Starting research: Improved mining
● Starts with mining facilities

### Roscosmos

Created after the fall of the Soviet Union, this Russian corporation has never reached the heights of their pioneering predecessors. Struggling with shrinking budgets, they pushed to commercialize their space program. Now backed with a resource extraction venture and excellent life support technology, they are ready to claw back their place at the front.

**Flavor traits (from new-game screen):**
● Starting research: Space Farming
● Starts with hydroponic farms
